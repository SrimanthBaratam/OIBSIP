Steve Jobs

1955 – 2011

“Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works.”

Biography

Steven Paul Jobs was an American entrepreneur, inventor, and business magnate. He was born on February 24, 1955, in San Francisco, California, and was adopted shortly after birth.

In 1976, Steve Jobs co-founded Apple along with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne. They introduced the Apple I and Apple II computers, helping popularize personal computing.

After leaving Apple in 1985, Jobs founded NeXT Inc. and later became the CEO of Pixar Animation Studios, producing iconic films like Toy Story and Finding Nemo.

Jobs returned to Apple in 1997 and led its historic comeback with products like the iMac, iPod, iPhone, and iPad.

Known for his perfectionism and creativity, Steve Jobs inspired the world with his belief in innovation, simplicity, and thinking differently.

Legacy

Steve Jobs passed away on October 5, 2011, but his ideas and products continue to shape modern technology and design.
